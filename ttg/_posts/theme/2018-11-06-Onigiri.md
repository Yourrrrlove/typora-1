Onigiri is a Typora theme for Japanese texts.

Onigiri uses [M PLUS 1p](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/M+PLUS+1p), which makes Japanese Kanji, Hiragana & Katakana quite a beauty.

*Designed and tested on macOS.*
